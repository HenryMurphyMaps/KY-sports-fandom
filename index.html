University of Kentucky Sports Fandom

This project shows the areas around the University of Kentuckys Campus with sports venues assocaited with the schools sports teams!

Left Column - Description

This project is done to showcase the areas around the University of Kentucky where the sports teams play. Kentuckys Campus offers so many oppertunites to dive into sports fandom with so many different sports and sports venues to pick from!

The goal of this project is to allow sports fans and non-sports fans to see where the oppertunites to go see their school compete are! This gives us insight spatially to see where the sports venues are distrubted around campus, allowing students and non students to see where they can go to watch their school preform!

Visualizations created from lidar data provided by KyFromAbove in ArcGIS Pro.

Page and visualizations created by Henry Murphy for GEO 409, Department of Geography, University of Kentucky. Spring 2024.
